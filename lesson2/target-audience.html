Target Audience

This site is ment to be for everyone who is interested in knowing the weather of the place where it is located, and it will also be a great tool for anyone who plans to travel and wants to know if their destination will have the best wheather to receive him/her. .Within the public interested in visiting this site we can include: students, journalists, fathers, mothers, travelers, tourists, etc.

Personas

Diana Restrepo

Diana Restrepo is a housewife who spends a large part of her day caring for her children. In the afternoons Diana goes to her children's school on foot to take them back home, but sometimes it is difficult for her as she does not foresee the heavy rains that appear quickly while she is crossing. This would not be a problem if Diana knew how the weather was in advance.

Juan Velez

Juan Velez is a 27-year-old adventurer who constantly travels from one place to another. He very much enjoys the moments that he lives in each place of arrival, but he admits that sometimes he does not wear the right clothes or accessories that his destination demands. Using this website can help Juan know what to expect on each trip and prepare accordingly.

Scenarios

A person works/studies near his/her house and walks every day toward there, but before leaving she/he wants to make sure that it will not rain while is on his/her way to his/her destination, and for that the nicewheather.co is a great option for it.

A radio announcer needs to quickly report what the weather will be like during the day, for that he uses visit coolweather.co and he/she can confidently break the news.

A family plans their summer vacations, and they want to see which place has the best weather to enjoy the beach/pool. This family can quickly and accurately consult coolweather.co and know first-hand which is the best option.

A school wants to go on a field trip to a nature reserve but they need to know which is the least cloudy day to see and enjoy the place. coolweather.co is ready to inform teachers and students how the weather will be in order to plan the best day of departure.

A farmer wants to buy a piece of land to grow vegetables, and he wants to know which is the closest place that has the best weather conditions to start his project. coolweather can show you the most common climates of the places near where he lives, in order to have the best information and finally make a good purchase
